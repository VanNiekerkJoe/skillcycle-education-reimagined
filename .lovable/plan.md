

# SkillCycle Website - Complete Redesign Plan

## Overview
A tech-forward, multi-page website for the 2026 Hult Competition that positions SkillCycle as an innovative solution addressing the global e-waste crisis while bringing quality digital education to underprivileged schools in South Africa.

---

## Design System
- **Primary Colors**: Navy (#1a2540) + Cyan (#00d4ff) from your logo
- **Style**: Clean, modern, tech-forward with subtle animations
- **Typography**: Bold headlines, clean body text for readability
- **Visual Elements**: Gradient accents, animated statistics counters, iconography

---

## Site Structure (5 Pages)

### 1. Home Page (Landing)
**Hero Section**
- Large animated headline: "Turning E-Waste Into Education"
- Compelling tagline about transforming discarded laptops into learning opportunities
- Two CTAs: "See How It Works" + "Partner With Us"
- Animated background with subtle tech patterns

**Quick Impact Stats (Animated counters)**
- 35% of people lack basic digital skills globally
- 18% of rural schools have internet access
- 87% of jobs require digital literacy
- Number of laptops you can potentially save

**Problem Preview**
- Visual comparison of urban vs rural digital access
- "Learn More" link to Problem page

**Solution Preview**
- Brief intro to SkillOS with visual mockup/illustration
- "Explore SkillOS" CTA

**Partners Logos Strip**
- Siyavula and other partner logos

---

### 2. The Problem Page
**E-Waste Crisis Section**
- Windows 10 obsolescence impact visualization
- Statistics on devices going to landfills
- Environmental consequences (CO2, toxic materials)

**Digital Divide Section**
- Interactive comparison: Urban (62% internet) vs Rural (18% internet)
- Infographics showing education gaps
- Your researched statistics presented visually:
  - Global digital literacy rates
  - Teacher preparedness (59% feel unprepared)
  - Employment impact

**Why This Matters**
- Connection between digital skills and future employment
- Economic impact on communities
- Call to action leading to Solution

---

### 3. Our Solution Page (SkillOS & StudentHub)
**SkillOS Overview**
- Interactive feature showcase with icons/illustrations:
  - Lightweight OS for older hardware
  - CAPS-aligned study materials
  - Quizzes, videos, books, educational games
  - Works completely offline

**Offline Server Technology**
- Visual diagram showing: Student PC → Teacher PC → Local Server
- Explanation of how it works without internet

**StudentHub Features**
- Student dashboard mockup showing:
  - Marks and grades tracking
  - Analytics showing improvement areas
  - Peer and teacher messaging
  - Teacher test/assignment uploads

**Educational Content**
- Partnership with Siyavula
- Types of materials available
- Curriculum alignment explanation
